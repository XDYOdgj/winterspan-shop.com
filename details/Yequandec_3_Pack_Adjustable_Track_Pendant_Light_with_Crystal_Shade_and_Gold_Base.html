Yequandec 3-Pack Adjustable Track Pendant Light

Elevate your interior with a modern and elegant lighting solution featuring a gold base and crystal shade.

Why Choose This Pendant Light?

This 3-pack track pendant light is designed to add both functionality and style to any room. Its adjustable cord and tilt mechanism allow you to customize the lighting angle and position to perfectly match your design vision. Whether you're working with sloped ceilings or track wall mounts, this fixture offers the flexibility and adaptability you need.

The J-type track adapter enables direct installation, making the setup process seamless and straightforward. You can also personalize the light with different wire colors and adapter types, ensuring it complements your existing decor.

Key Features

  • Adjustable tilt and cord length for flexible positioning and tailored lighting.
  • Universal bulb base E26 (bulb not included) for easy compatibility with your preferred lighting bulbs.
  • Sleek J-type track adapter for hassle-free, direct installation.
  • Customizable wire length, color, and adapter type to suit your interior design and layout.
  • Easy installation without the need for a professional electrician.

Enhance any space with a touch of sophistication and modern elegance. Ideal for use in homes, restaurants, and bars, this pendant light delivers a seamless blend of style and practicality.
