SKIVTGLAMP Adjustable Retro Pendant Lights

Elevate your space with a timeless blend of modern utility and retro charm. These adjustable pendant lights are designed to complement any room in your home or any setting in your restaurant. Their sleek black and white industrial halo aesthetics add a touch of sophistication, making them a perfect match for kitchens, dining areas, bars, and more.

With a dimmable feature powered by an included dimmer switch and capable of using up to 60W max bulb, these lights offer the perfect balance of illumination and ambiance. Whether you're looking to make a bold statement or simply enhance your lighting setup, these pendants are engineered for simplicity, efficiency, and elegance.
